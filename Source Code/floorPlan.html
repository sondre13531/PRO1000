Floor plan

As you can see, the USNStart team has fully fleshed out 500 square meters of collaborative coworking space. Made for students and professionals alike, we offer a wide range of amenities to help foster innovation and productivity, while prioritizing individual comfort.

Zones and Amenities

The facility is split up into 7 sections, each serving an important function.

  1. Open Workspace: This is the heart of a coworking space, featuring flexible seating arrangements with communal desks, tables, and chairs. It's suitable for individual work, informal meetings, and collaborative projects. There is space for 60 individuals working here.
  2. Private offices: 6 small, fully-furnished offices that can accommodate individuals or small teams, offering more privacy and a dedicated workspace.
  3. Meeting rooms: 5 meeting rooms of varying size equipped with projectors and whiteboards for presentations, client meetings, or team discussions.
  4. Lounge Area: Comfortable seating areas, providing a relaxed atmosphere for informal meetings or relaxation.
  5. Kitchen and Dining Area: A well-equipped kitchenette with facilities for preparing meals and dining. It also offers complimentary coffee, tea, and snacks.
  6. Printing and Scanning area: Equipment for printing, scanning, and photocopying documents.
  7. Game room: A recreational space with games like billiards, ping-pong, and video games, encouraging breaks and social interaction.
